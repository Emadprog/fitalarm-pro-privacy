Delete Your Account & Data – FitAlarm Pro

You can delete your account and all associated data permanently using the options below:

Delete from Inside the App

  • Open FitAlarm Pro
  • Go to Settings
  • Tap My Account
  • Tap Delete Account

Once deleted, your account and all related data stored in Firebase will be permanently and completely removed.

All local data stored on the device will also be removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data.
