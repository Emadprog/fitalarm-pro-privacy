Delete Your Account & Data – FitAlarm Pro

You can delete your account and all associated data permanently using the steps below:

Delete from Inside the App

  • Open FitAlarm Pro
  • Go to Settings
  • Tap My Account
  • Tap Delete Account

Once deleted, your account data stored in Firebase will be permanently removed.
